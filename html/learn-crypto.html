Introduction To Cryptocurrency

Written by Daniel Samuel

What is cryptocurrency?

"Cryptocurrency" is more than just digital money—it's a revolutionary financial system powered by blockchain technology, where trust is built into the code, not a central authority. It's a borderless, secure, and transparent way to own, trade, and invest in value, redefining how we think about money in a world driven by innovation.

Brief history of Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency began in 2009 with the launch of Bitcoin, created by the mysterious figure Satoshi Nakamoto. Bitcoin introduced the concept of decentralized digital currency, allowing peer-to-peer transactions without the need for banks. Over the years, thousands of other cryptocurrencies have emerged, each with unique features, but Bitcoin remains the pioneering force.

What does market capitalization mean?

Market cap (short for market capitalization) is the total value of a cryptocurrency. It’s calculated by multiplying the current price of one coin by the total number of coins in circulation.

Formula: Market Cap = Current Price × Circulating Supply

What is the price?

This is the current value of one unit of a cryptocurrency in the market.

  • It shows how much you would need to buy one coin or how much you would get if you sold one coin.
  • Example: If Bitcoin's price is $30,000, it means one Bitcoin is worth $30,000.

What does 24h change mean?

The percentage change in the cryptocurrency's price over the last 24 hours.

  • It shows how much the price has increased or decreased in the past day.
  • Positive change (e.g., +5%) means the price went up, while negative change (e.g., -3%) means it went down.

What is 24-Hour Volume?

This is the total value of all transactions (buys and sells) made with the cryptocurrency in the last 24 hours.

  • It measures how actively the coin is being traded.
  • Higher volume indicates more activity, which can mean greater interest or market movement.

How to Get Started with Crypto

#1:

Creating a wallet

A crypto wallet is a digital tool that allows you to store, send, and receive cryptocurrency securely. It works by storing your private keys, which are essential for accessing and managing your crypto assets.There are two types of walle; Hot wallet and Cold wallet

Wallet Type Description Pros Cons
Hot Wallet Internet-connected wallet Easy to use, quick access Less secure, prone to hacking
Cold Wallet Offline wallet Very secure, hack-proof Less convenient, requires setup

#2:

How to set up a wallet (Hot wallet)

  1. Download a wallet app (e.g., MetaMask, Trust Wallet).
  2. Follow the on-screen instructions to create a new wallet.
  3. Write down your seed phrase and store it securely.
  4. Confirm your seed phrase and start using your wallet.

#3:

Buying your first crypto

  1. Choose an exchange (e.g., Binance, Coinbase).
  2. Create an account and verify your identity.
  3. Deposit funds using your preferred payment method.
  4. Buy your desired cryptocurrency.
  5. Transfer the crypto to your wallet for safekeeping.

Ways to secure your wallet

  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on your exchange account.
  • Use strong, unique passwords.
  • Regularly update your security settings.

Learn The Basics Of Crypto Trading

Crypto trading involves buying and selling cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum to make a profit. Trades happen on exchanges, where prices change based on supply and demand. Traders use strategies like day trading, swing trading, or holding long-term. Risk management is key—only invest what you can afford to lose and set stop-loss limits. Staying informed through market analysis, including news and price charts, helps in making better decisions.

We shall discuss the types of Crypto Trades below.... stay with me.....

01

Spot Trading

Spot trading is a straightforward method of buying or selling financial assets like cryptocurrencies, stocks, or commodities at the current market price, known as the spot price. In this type of trading, transactions are settled instantly, meaning the buyer receives the asset and the seller gets paid right away.

02

Futures Trading

Futures trading involves agreeing to buy or sell an asset at a specific price on a future date. You don’t own the asset; instead, you trade contracts tied to its price. It allows leverage, meaning you can trade with more funds than you have, increasing both potential profits and risks.

03

Margin Trading

Margin trading involves borrowing funds from the exchange to trade larger amounts than your actual balance. You use your funds as collateral, and profits or losses are magnified due to the borrowed amount. If losses exceed your collateral, the exchange may liquidate your position.

Earning With Crypto

Earning with crypto can be done through staking, where you earn rewards for holding coins, yield farming by providing liquidity to DeFi platforms, or mining, where you validate transactions and earn new coins. Each method offers potential rewards, but it's important to understand the risks involved.

Staking

Staking involves holding a cryptocurrency in a wallet to support the operations of a blockchain network, like validating transactions. In return, you earn rewards, usually in the form of more tokens. It benefits the network by securing it and helps you earn passive income with minimal effort.

Yield Farming

Yield farming involves providing liquidity to decentralized finance (DeFi) platforms in exchange for rewards. You can earn interest or tokens by lending your crypto to others or by participating in liquidity pools. It requires active management but can offer high returns.

Mining

Mining is the process of using computational power to validate transactions on a blockchain. Miners solve complex mathematical problems, and in return, they earn new coins or tokens. Mining is essential for maintaining the security and integrity of decentralized networks like Bitcoin.

What is the best crypto for beginners?

The best crypto for beginners is Bitcoin (BTC) or Ethereum (ETH) because they are popular, reliable, and easy to use. Stablecoins like USDT are also good for low risk. Always do research before investing!

How much money do I need to start?

You don’t need a lot to get started! On our platform, you can begin with as little as $10, making it easy for anyone to invest. Whether you’re a beginner or just testing the waters, we recommend starting with an amount you’re comfortable with. This way, you can learn the basics of cryptocurrency trading without feeling pressured. Remember, investing is a journey—start small, learn, and grow at your own pace!

Is cryptocurrency legal in my country?

The legality of cryptocurrency depends on where you live. In many countries, it’s legal to buy, sell, and trade cryptocurrencies, but some have restrictions or bans. To be sure, check your country’s specific regulations on cryptocurrency. If you’re unsure, consult a local expert or trusted resource to stay compliant with the law.

How do I report crypto taxes?

To report crypto taxes, track all your transactions, calculate your gains or losses, and declare any crypto income as required by your country’s tax laws. Use tax software or consult a professional for help if needed.
